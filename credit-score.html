What is a Credit Score?

A credit score (also called CIBIL score) is a 3-digit number that represents your creditworthiness. It ranges from 300 to 900. A higher score increases your chances of loan and credit card approval.

Why is Credit Score Important?

  • Faster loan approvals
  • Lower interest rates
  • Higher credit limits
  • Better financial credibility
